Hi there!

My name is Mary and I am just starting to learn how to code. I am excited to be learning a new, invaluable skill!

I am originally from Northern California, but I've lived in Seattle for the past four years.

My personal life is pretty low key. I enjoy spending time in my apartment with my partner and our cat, reading or listening to audiobooks, dabbling in film photography, and trying new restaurants. Of course, I lose a lot of time to TikTok too, but I am constantly impressed by the variety of content and remain unashamed of my addiction.

In the spring, I will be starting the UW Certificate for UX and Visual Interface Design, which I am really looking forward to. I have been thinking about transitioning from my current role for a while, or at least pondering what my next move would be, and when one of my mentors suggested I would excel as a UX Designer, I had to look up what that even meant. Since learning about it though, I realized how many of the aspects of my job that I like are related to creating the best experience, in both visual and interactive elements. So, upon deciding to pursue the UX Certificate, I decided I might as well dive right in and learn how to code as well.


My plans

One of the things I would like to do as I learn to code is to surprise my partner with a website for his photography. He has shared his interest in both film and digital photography with me and has talked about setting up a portfolio to share and sell his work. He is in school to become a Physical Therapist and doesn't have much time for working on this himself.

Another way I would like to use my new skills is to take over managing the website of a non-profit my mom has been part of for a long time. I have been handling the formatting and editing of their newsletter for years, and my mom has even asked me about helping update their website. It sounds like the Board of the organization is mostly older, non-tech savvy folks and the person they currently have "managing" their website isn't as attentive or helpful as they would like. I would love to be able to bring their website up to par and help them expand their organization to a younger crowd!
